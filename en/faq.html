Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to move forward with the 7-day demo or the DigitalSuite plan.

What does the 7-day demo include?

It includes an AI Agent for WhatsApp with predefined answers, configured after a call to understand your business.

What is not included in the demo?

No automations or social media integration; it is focused on WhatsApp only.

What is the cost after the trial?

After the trial, the service continues at $47.77 per month.

How long does setup take?

Setup is typically completed within a few days after the initial call and information handoff.

Do I need an existing CRM?

It is not required for the demo. For higher plans, we can integrate or implement DigitalSuite.

What integrations are available?

Depending on the plan, we integrate CRM, marketing tools, internal automations, and voice agents.

How is data privacy handled?

We apply security practices and controlled access to keep operational data protected.

Can I scale to DigitalSuite after the demo?

Yes, the demo is designed as an initial step toward DigitalSuite with an organized transition.

Does the demo include voice agents?

No. Voice agents are available in higher plans like DigitalSuite.

Is there support during the demo?

We provide support to ensure proper activation and follow-up for the WhatsApp flow.

What does my team need to start?

Product information, FAQ content, and a point person for the initial call.

How do I schedule a diagnosis call?

You can do so via the contact form or by requesting the demo through WhatsApp.
