WhatsApp AI Sales Sprint: A practical 7-day launch

A fast WhatsApp launch starts with clarity: what to answer, when to hand off to your team, and how to measure progress. An AI Agent with predefined answers can deliver speed while preserving your brand tone.

Define your five most frequent inquiries and craft short, clear, action-oriented responses.

2. Design the next step

Decide when to book a call or send a summary with relevant commercial details.

3. Set follow-up timing

Review conversions, response times, and qualification rates to refine your script.
